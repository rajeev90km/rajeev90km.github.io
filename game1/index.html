Game1: Frostbite

Designed by: Rajeev Mukundan

Frostbite is a survival game similar to the popular "Don't Starve". Survive the winter for as long as possible in the wilderness with limited resources available. Stay warm and healthy while defending yourself from the wild.

Gameplay

The world is divided into three screens

  • The player controls the position of the main character with the arrow keys.
  • The player has 2 characteristics: health and body temperature. Both keep decreasing steadily.
  • The objective of the game is to stay warm and healthy for as long as possible. If either of the 2 characteristics reaches zero, the player dies.
  • The player is equipped with an axe. Use X to use the axe to cut trees or to attack the wild animals.
  • Once you chop trees, the player gets lumber. Use F to start a fire for a while using the obtained lumber . Fire increases body temperature a bit.
  • Wild Animals can attack you when you get close to them. Once you kill them using the axe, press E to eat the meat. This increases health a bit
  • Once you get lumber/meat, you can press C to carry them with you. Carry it to the wizard at the end of the forest to trade it for warm clothing(temperature drops slowly for a while)/replenish health(restore 75% of health).
  • Trees once cut, will grow back at a slow rate.
