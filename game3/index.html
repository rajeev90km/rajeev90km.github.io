Game3: Cube Volleyball

Designed by: Rajeev Mukundan

This is simple arcade volleyball game played two cubes.

Gameplay

  • The player to reach 10 points first wins.
  • Player 1 can be controlled by WASD keys. W to jump, A and D to move left and right.
  • Player 2 can be controlled by arrow keys. Up Arrow to jump, Left and Right arrows to move.
  • If the ball hits the net, the other player gains a point.
  • A player can bounce the ball not more than 4 times. The other player gains a point otherwise.
  • At the start of each round, the ball spawns from above one of the player and falls down (influence of gravity. No external forces).
